About BRGR

BRGR has established itself as a go-to spot for a quick, delicious bite. In the years since, the operations have expanded to include additional trucks along the coast and a moving service between Sodic, New Giza, and Tagamoa in Cairo, and catering services for events and parties. We were excited to see BRGR’s first brick and mortar store in Mall of Arabia’s new phase, and quickly headed there for our BRGR fix!
